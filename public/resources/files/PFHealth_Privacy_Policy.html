Price First Health Privacy Policy

 

Effective date: April 4th, 2017

 

Price First Health, Inc. (“Price First Health”) takes your privacy very seriously. The following Privacy Policy (the “Policy”) reviews how we collect, use, and disclose your information in the course of offering the Price First Health website and its related contents and services (the “Services”). This Policy is intended to inform you about how we treat the personal information, or information relating to an individual who is or could be identified from that information you provide us. If you do not agree to any part of this Policy, then do not use the Services.

 

1.       Information Gathered by Price First Health

a.       We receive, store, and process information, that you make available to us when accessing or using our Services, including when you:

                                                               i.      fill in any form used by the Services, such as when you register or update the details of your user account, or when you supply ID and other verification information;

                                                             ii.      post content to the Services, including but not limited to listings for apartments;

                                                            iii.      apply to listings, post comments or reviews, or use the Services to communicate with other people who use our Services;

                                                           iv.      link your account on a third-party social network site (e.g., Facebook) to your Price First Health Account, in which case we will obtain what information that the third-party social network site allows us to receive through its application protocol interface or other moderated access, to the extent allowed by your settings on that third-party service; and

                                                             v.      communicate with Price First Health.

 

b.      We may also receive, store, and process information that is automatically recorded by our servers whenever you access or use the Platform, regardless of whether you are registered with Price First Health or logged in to your Price First Health Account, such as your IP Address, the date and time you access or use the Platform, HTML header information, the hardware and software you are using, referring and exit pages and URLs, the number of clicks, device event information, the geographic location from which you access the Services, pages viewed and the order of those pages, and the amount of time spent on particular pages.

 

c.       We may also use "cookies" or other automated mechanisms to collect information and personalize your service. Cookies are small files that services send to your browser for storage on your computer. They make use of the Services by saving preferences, remembering your login information, and recalling other aspects of your use of the service. While most browsers allow you to disable cookies, we recommend that you leave cookies enabled so as not to interfere with the full functionality of the Services.

 

2.       How Price First Health Uses and Processes Your Information

a.       We generally use, store and process information about you in order to operate and enable you to access and use the Services, and all of the features therein. We also use this information to:

                                                               i.      to operate, protect, improve and optimize the Services, our business, and our users’ experience, such as to perform analytics, conduct research, personalize or otherwise customize your experience, and to provide customer service;

                                                             ii.      to send you service, support and administrative messages, reminders, technical notices, updates, security alerts, and information requested by you;

                                                            iii.      to help create and maintain a trusted and safer environment when using the Services, including through detection and prevention of actual and potential fraud and other harmful activity, conducting investigations and risk assessments, enforcing our Terms of Use, verifying the address of your listings, verifying any identifications provided by you, and conducting checks against databases and information sources (such as, but not limited to, public government databases) for fraud detection and prevention, risk assessment and harm prevention purposes. In this regard, we may do any or all of the foregoing with or without further notifying you;

                                                           iv.      to comply with our legal obligations, resolve any disputes that we may have with any of our users, and enforce our agreements with companies not related by common ownership or control (“Third Parties”).

 

Members of the Price First Health staff will have access to user information to the extent it is needed for these purposes.

 

b.      We may, either directly or through Third Parties, use automated methods to or manually review, scan, or analyze your communications with other users exchanged via the Services for fraud prevention, risk assessment, regulatory compliance, investigation, product development, research and customer support purposes. We may also scan, review or analyze messages for research and product development purposes to help make searches, bookings and user communications more efficient and effective, as well as to improve and expand product offerings.

 

c.       We will not review, scan, or analyze your communications for sending Third-Party marketing messages to you. We will also not sell these reviews or analyses of communications to Third Parties nor will we sell any of our user's information to outside outlets or vendors.

 

3.       Disclosure or Sharing of Information You Post to the Site

a.       Price First Health will make available to the general public any information you submit through the Services that is intended for publication on the Services, including but not limited to your name and public contact information, descriptions and content used for apartment listings, and public communications you make on the platform. When you express an interest in renting an apartment, Price First Health may also share your information with the owner and/or current tenant of that apartment to the extent practicable to facilitate that transaction, and vice versa.

 

b.      If you create an account on or access Price First Health through a third-party social networking site, we may receive, disclose, and make publicly available information that such third-party site allows us to use pursuant to their application protocol interface or other method of interoperability. Limits and conditions on such disclosure are exclusively set by you and the relevant third-party social networking site.

c.       We may share your information with third party service providers used by Price First Health to operate, research, or improve the Services. Price First Health shares user information with such providers solely as is needed for those parties to provide services to Price First Health.

 

d.      We reserve the right to access, preserve and disclose your account information and content if required to do so by law or in a good faith belief that such access, preservation or disclosure is reasonably necessary (a) to comply with legal processes; (b) to enforce and administer our Terms of Use and this Policy with users; (c) for fraud prevention, risk assessment, and investigation; or (d) to protect the rights, property or personal safety of Price First Health users.

 

e.      Price First Health may request users to provide payment information (such as credit card number and expiration date) to process fees. Payment information will be used only to send to our credit card processor, for the purposes of collecting payment for Services rendered or to be rendered. We would not make this information visible to anyone other than our credit card processor nor communicate it over a non-encrypted connection.
